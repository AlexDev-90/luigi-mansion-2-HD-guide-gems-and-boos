Luigi's Mansion 2 HD - Gem Locations

Gloomy Manor

Mansion Overview: There are thirteen Amethysts to be found in the Gloomy Manor. Since your Poltergust 5000 isn't complete at the start of the game, you will have to replay the first mission once you unlock the Stroboscope to collect the Gems! (These locations also work for Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon on the 3DS.)

Amethyst 1 - Mudroom

Available in: A-1 Poltergust 5000

Peek through the window in the Mudroom to see the Polterpup dig up an Amethyst and toss it into a nearby tree. Once you unlock the Stroboscope, flash the door in the Mudroom to get outside and suck the Gem out of the tree to collect it.

Amethyst 2 - Yard

Available in: A-1 Poltergust 5000

Leave the porch and head to the left of the stairs where several flowers have been planted. Give them a burst of light from your Stroboscope and they will bloom to reveal some cash and an Amethyst!

Amethyst 3 - Parlor

Available in: A-2 Gear Up

The grandfather clock in the Parlor has a green light on its display, indicating that it can be flashed. Use the Stroboscope to reveal an Amethyst.

Amethyst 4 - Studio

Available in: A-2 Gear Up

Stop in the Studio to look into the camera and you'll notice a cleverly hidden Gem on the other side of the window. Step behind the window curtain and nab it with your Poltergust.

Amethyst 5 - Entrance

Available in: A-3 Quiet Please

This is a very easy Gem to miss—rattle the suit of armor on the right for an explosion of money as well as a helmet! In the top left corner is a seemingly innocent portrait of a helmet. Suck up the helmet and shoot it at the portrait to reveal the Gem.

Amethyst 6 - Dining Room

Available in: A-3 Quiet Please

Before entering the dumbwaiter in the Dining Room, look at the ceiling to spy a safe. Flash the safe with your Stroboscope to find the next Gem.

Amethyst 7 - Kitchen

Available in: A-3 Quiet Please

Open the fridge to find a Gem encased in ice. Suck it up and bring it towards the stove to thaw it out—just make sure you clear out the Greenies first!

Amethyst 8 - Foyer

Available in: A-4 Visual Tricks

Walk to the left side of the garden and shine the Dark-Light on the wall opposite the merry-go-round to reveal another door. Enter it to go under the stairs, flash the gold spiders for cash, and finally take the back left door to wind up behind the aquarium.

Amethyst 9 - Patio

Available in: A-4 Visual Tricks

As you head back out onto the Patio, notice the two Cheep Cheep Statues. Shine your Dark-Light to reveal a third statue, which will spit out another Gem.

Amethyst 10 - Library

Available in: A-4 Visual Tricks

Instead of going to the Dining Room, take a detour to the Library. Shine your Dark-Light near the bookcase to reveal a globe, then stick it in your Poltergust and melt it in the fireplace to reveal a Gem.

Amethyst 11 - Study

Available in: A-5 Sticky Situation

Suck up the curtains to the left of where you found the gear in the Study. Shine the Dark-Light on the painting of the Gem to make it appear.

Amethyst 12 - Attic

Available in: A-5 Sticky Situation

Before cleaning out the cobwebs, head to the far right side to find a dresser with a Gem attached to the top.

Amethyst 13 - Bedroom

Available in: A-5 Sticky Situation

Activate the stove to start a fire in the Bedroom and spin the ceiling fan with your Poltergust. The ball of web hanging on the fan will spin into the web that was blocking you from picking up the last Gem. Head behind the divider to collect it!

Haunted Towers

Mansion Overview: There are thirteen Emeralds to be found in the Haunted Towers. These locations also work for Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon on the 3DS.

Emerald Locations

Emerald 1 - Tower Lobby

Available in: B-1 A Job for a Plumber

Run behind the stairs in the Tower Lobby to find a hidden entrance leading to the Gem.

Emerald 2 - Laboratory

Available in: B-1 A Job for a Plumber

While floating around the Laboratory on your bubble plant, aim for one of the large tubes near the ground floor. Drop in and look for the Gem trapped in a cobweb at the top right. Grab it and use the bubble plant to get back out.

Emerald 3 - Garden

Available in: B-2 The Pinwheel Gate

After dealing with the ghosts in the Garden, fill a nearby bucket with water, use it to grow the flowers along the right, and flash the flower near the bottom to receive the Gem.

Emerald 4 - Botany Lab

Available in: B-2 The Pinwheel Gate

Before revealing the invisible pipes in the Botany Lab, put a bucket under the missing piece. Flash the machine to make it drip water, then take it to the back corner plants, water them, and flash them with your Poltergust to reveal the Gem.

Emerald 5 - Seedling Lab

Available in: B-2 The Pinwheel Gate

Head to the Seedling Lab and use the potato shooter. A mouse carrying a Gem will appear—shoot flasks and pots to get a clear shot and reclaim the Gem.

Emerald 6 - West Garden

Available in: B-3 Graveyard Shift

Return to the Tower Lobby to reveal invisible pipes leading to a doormat near the base of the stairs. Turn on a water pipe to grow a spiky plant; shoot it at the Venus Flytrap to open the chest containing the Gem.

Emerald 7 - Hollow Tree

Available in: B-3 Graveyard Shift

Before heading to the Tree Roots, light a ball of web on fire, head to an alcove, destroy the webs to reveal a bucket, fill it in the nearby pool, and water a plant to reveal the Gem.

Emerald 8 - Old Cemetery

Available in: B-3 Graveyard Shift

Find the Gem waiting on a vine just after entering the Cemetery. Suck it up with your Poltergust.

Emerald 9 - Skybridge

Available in: B-4 Pool Party

Skip the Plant Nursery, move right to the Sky Bridge, and dodge the flowers. The last flower will reveal the Emerald.

Emerald 10 - Rumpus Room

Available in: B-4 Pool Party

Run around the creepy doll anticlockwise to screw its head off, revealing the Gem.

Emerald 11 - Solarium

Available in: B-4 Pool Party

After entering the Solarium, use the Dark-Light on an empty table near the stairs to reveal a hidden chest and the Gem inside.

Emerald 12 - Conservatory

Available in: B-4 Pool Party

Use the bubble plant to float up to the left in the Conservatory, shine the Dark-Light to reveal a door, then follow the counter-clockwise path through the minigame to collect the Gem.

Emerald 13 - Bathroom

Available in: B-4 Pool Party

Use the Dark-Light on the bathroom to reveal the final Emerald.

Old Clockworks

Mansion Overview: There are thirteen Rubies hidden within the Old Clockworks. These gems are tucked away among the machinery and secret passages.

Ruby Locations

Ruby 1 - Clockwork Court

Available in: C-1 A Timely Entrance

Follow the railroad tracks to the right until you reach a pile of rubble. Flash the bomb to clear it and reveal the hidden chest with the Dark-Light.

Ruby 2 - Warehouse

Available in: C-1 A Timely Entrance

Shine the Dark-Light at the boxes along the back wall of the Warehouse to uncover a hidden green ribbon. Unravel it to reveal the Gem.

Ruby 3 - Clock Tower Gate

Available in: C-1 A Timely Entrance

Flash near the back of the raised platform at the Clock Tower Gate to uncover a chest containing the Gem.

Ruby 4 - Storm Cellar

Available in: C-2 Underground Expedition

Next to the bed in the Storm Cellar, suck away the large cloth to reveal a painting of a Gem. Flash it with the Dark-Light to reveal the Gem.

Ruby 5 - Canyon Stairs

Available in: C-2 Underground Expedition

Head to the Canyon Stairs and look for the Ruby near the ceiling. Suck it up with your Poltergust.

Ruby 6 - Quarry

Available in: C-2 Underground Expedition

Sneak around the Quarry before pulling the mine cart to flood it with sand. Find a cave housing the Gem.

Ruby 7 - Clockmaker's Chambers

Available in: C-3 Roundhouse Brawl

Trigger the alarm to wake the Greenie in Clockmaker's Chambers; he will drop the Gem he was holding.

Ruby 8 - Roundhouse Upper

Available in: C-3 Roundhouse Brawl

Head down the second set of beams in the Roundhouse to find a chest containing a Gem.

Ruby 9 - Roundhouse Lower

Available in: C-3 Roundhouse Brawl

Before the boss battle in the Roundhouse, move left at the end of the stairs into an alcove to collect the Gem.

Ruby 10 - Gear Chambers

Available in: C-5 Piece at Last

Climb the boxes, reveal a bridge with the Dark-Light, and enter the pocket dimension minigame to collect the Gem after bouncing on gears.

Ruby 11 - Maintenance Hub

Available in: C-5 Piece at Last

While taking the lift across, drop to the bottom floor, follow the arrows to a toolbox, and reveal the Gem.

Ruby 12 - Service Elevator

Available in: C-5 Piece at Last

The mirror near the Service Elevator is covered by cloth. Toad can retrieve the Gem on the opposite side when punted over.

Ruby 13 - Movements

Available in: C-Boss Showtime

The final Gem lies outside a window on the left side of Movements. Suck it up with the Poltergust on your way to the boss battle.

Secret Mine

Mansion Overview: There are thirteen Sapphires to be found in the Secret Mine. These locations also work for Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon on the 3DS.

Sapphire Locations

Sapphire 1 - Smokehouse

Available in: D-1 Cold Case

Use your Dark-Light to reveal the missing towel rack on the back wall of the Smokehouse, then suck both towels off to reveal a secret alcove.

Sapphire 2 - Fishing Hut

Available in: D-1 Cold Case

There is a small gap in the floorboards near the front left of the Fishing Hut that hides the Gem. Use your Poltergust to suck the Gem out. (Collect this Gem before releasing Toad from his portrait prison.)

Sapphire 3 - Old Mine

Available in: D-1 Cold Case

There’s a peephole against the bottom wall giving you a view of the Gem. Throw Toad down the shaft so he appears at the bottom with the Gem.

Sapphire 4 - High Wires

Available in: D-1 Cold Case

Head north to the High Wires area and hitch a ride on the zip line to a platform. A Gem sits in a nest—suck it up with your Poltergust.

Sapphire 5 - Airway

Available in: D-1 Cold Case

Peek through the binoculars by the scales in the Airway to see a giant frozen mouth blowing cold air. A Gem lies inside but is snatched by a bat and dumped in a barrel. Retrieve it from the other side.

Sapphire 6 - Chalet Approach

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

Head outside to the Chalet Approach. The grill near the door has a whole chicken on it. Activate the grill, set the chicken on fire, then bring it down the path to a frozen portrait of the Gem. Melt it and reveal the Gem with the Dark-Light.

Sapphire 7 - Prospector's Crossing

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

Make a detour through the Basin toward Prospector's Crossing. A Gem is buried under the ice—shine the Dark-Light at the pickaxe, spin it to reveal wood blocks, then place logs on the ice to break it and collect the Gem.

Sapphire 8 - Skip Slope

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

Slide down the Skip Slope, angle Luigi, and slip through the wheel on the left path. It may take a few tries, but the Gem is worth it.

Sapphire 9 - Shaft Lower

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

At the bottom of the shaft, tug the rope and move the platform up to reveal the uncovered Gem.

Sapphire 10 - Deep Hall

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

Grab a hot rock and aim it near the ceiling to melt the ice trapping the Gem.

Sapphire 11 - Crossroads

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

After going through the first portal, reveal a hidden box where you found the frozen key. Collect the Spirit Balls, then return through the portal to collect the Gem.

Sapphire 12 - Chalet

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

Enter the portal in the Crystal Quarry to fall onto the couch, freeing the Gem from its hiding place.

Sapphire 13 - High Wires

Available in: D-2 Hit Rock Bottom

While using the hot coal shooter at the High Wires, aim one of the coals at the half-frozen tree on the left to make it fall. This leads to a pocket dimension minigame where you collect the Gem.

Treacherous Mansion

Mansion Overview: There are thirteen Diamonds hidden in the Treacherous Mansion. These locations also work for Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon on the 3DS.

Diamond Locations

Diamond 1 - Underground Lab

Available in: E-1 Front-Door Key

Spin the wheel the Greenies were playing with in the Underground Lab to electrocute a suit of armor and release a Boo. As the Boo emerges, the helmet pops off—use it to smash the jars around the room, one of which contains the Gem.

Diamond 2 - Haunted Catacombs

Available in: E-1 Front-Door Key

Before following the Polterpup's tracks in the Haunted Catacombs, inspect the suit of armor with the sword. It will point you toward a corridor; follow the direction, reveal a portrait of a ball of web and fire, then bring the flaming ball back to the wall of web to reveal the Gem.

Diamond 3 - Cliffside

Available in: E-1 Front-Door Key

Follow the pointing suits of armor until you reach a corridor with Diamond 2, then move to the front and take the door on the right to find the Gem hanging from a vine.

Diamond 4 - Front Entrance

Available in: E-2 Double Trouble

Cast the Dark-Light on the gargoyle at the right side of the stairs to reveal a chain. Pull it to reveal a hidden safe under the stairs. After defeating an Empowered Slammer, claim the Gem.

Diamond 5 - Restrooms

Available in: E-2 Double Trouble

In Grand East, find the door leading to the Restrooms. Shine the Dark-Light on the wall to the left, yank the chain on the toilet, and collect the Gem that pops out!

Diamond 6 - Gargoyle Roof

Available in: E-2 Double Trouble

Yank on the Mammoth's trunk in the Ice Age Exhibit to be shot up to the Gargoyle Roof. Complete the constellation by shooting stars toward it, and collect the Gem.

Diamond 7 - Ancient Exhibit

Available in: E-2 Double Trouble

Fill a bucket with water from the Dark Age Exhibit and take it to the Ancient Exhibit. Climb the long path to the main level to find a seedling near a tree, water it, and receive the Gem.

Diamond 8 - Aviation Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Pull one of the sandbags on the hot air balloon. An Empowered Hider and two Greenies will appear. Capture them, then pull the other sandbag to reveal a Gem.

Diamond 9 - Nautical Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Activate the portal in the Nautical Exhibit, grab a log from the Jungle Exhibit, dump it on the platform to lower the helm blocking the door, and enter to find a chest with the Gem.

Diamond 10 - Dark Age Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Retrieve a Balloon Berry from the Study and enter the portal to the Front Entrance. Float up to a small landing with a gargoyle holding a blue book, drop the book, and reveal the Gem.

Diamond 11 - Jungle Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Enter the portal to the Jungle Exhibit with a Balloon Berry, float to the upper level, and collect coins in time to be rewarded with the Gem.

Diamond 12 - Ice Age Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Enter the portal to the Ice Age Exhibit with a Balloon Berry, float up, follow the coins, and collect them all to reveal a chest with the Gem.

Diamond 13 - Train Exhibit

Available in: E-3 A Train to Catch

Use the Dark-Light on the floor to reveal missing tracks. A large train will enter the room; step onto it and collect the Gem.
